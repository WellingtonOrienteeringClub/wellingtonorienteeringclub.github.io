Orienteering Wellington provides you with great orienteering experiences across our region.

Orienteering is a family of navigational sports where participants use a map and compass. It is suitable for people of all ages, experience and abilities, and is great fun for families. Through orienteering you will get to discover farms, forests, dunes and complex urban environments. Visit New to orienteering? to find out more!

Connect with us on Facebook, come along to one of our Tuesday training sessions, or meet us at one of our events. Everyone is welcome!
