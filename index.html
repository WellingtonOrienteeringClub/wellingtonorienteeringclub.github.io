Welcome to the website for Orienteering Wellington.

Orienteering Wellington organises navigation-sport events across the Wellington region.

This includes classic and sprint orienteering events, as well as rogaines. Not sure what these are, or what to do when you get to an event? Visit our New to orienteering? page to find out more!

Connect with us on Facebook, come along to one of our Tuesday training sessions, or meet us at one of our events. Everyone is welcome!
